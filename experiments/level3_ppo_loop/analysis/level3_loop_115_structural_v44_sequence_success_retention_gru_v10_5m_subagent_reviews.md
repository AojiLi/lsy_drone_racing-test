## W&B / PPO Diagnostics

Recommendation: reject v44 as a structural/data-distribution failure, not as a
PPO instability failure.

Evidence:

- Retention was active: the dataset loaded `7761` samples from `24` sequences.
- `retention/sampled_batch_size` stayed at `512`.
- Train-log retention improved:
  `teacher_kl 23.48 -> 20.05`,
  `teacher_action_mse 0.0392 -> 0.0232`,
  `teacher_agreement 0.680 -> 0.756`.
- PPO did not show classic blow-up: approximate KL stayed low, clip fraction
  stayed at `0`, and entropy did not collapse.
- Critic signal remained weak, with explained variance near zero.
- Race curves did not convert: finished rate stayed `0`, passed-gate rate
  stayed tiny, and hard eval stayed at `0%` success for all milestones.

Diagnosis: v44 fixed loop114's inactive-retention bug, but successful
train-pool sequence rehearsal did not produce robust normal-start first-gate
behavior on validation seeds.

## Structure / Research Synthesis

Recommendation: launch a new named structural lane rather than continuing v44.

Local evidence:

- v40/v42/v43/v44 are now a consistent negative sequence for GRU/v10:
  wiring is clean, but normal-start gate acquisition does not emerge.
- loop107/v37 1M remains the frontier with `21%` success, `1.66` mean gates,
  `79%` crash, and `7.578s` mean successful time.
- loop110/v39 3M tied the `21%`/`79%` frontier with faster successful time
  and different solved seeds, suggesting useful v5 frontier behavior remains
  available even though seed coverage churns.

Recommended lane:

```text
v45_v5_frontier_seed_union_retention_mlp_from_loop110_3m
```

Use the stronger v5 MLP/frontier path instead of the failed GRU/v10 path.
Start from loop110/v39 3M and use training-only retention/distillation from
successful disjoint train-pool rollouts generated by compatible v5 MLP frontier
teachers. Deployment remains a single PPO Actor; no teacher, ensemble, planner,
rule controller, shield, or fallback is used at inference.

## Main-Agent Synthesis

The reviewers agree that v44 should be rejected as executed. It is useful
scientifically because it proves recurrent sequence retention is wired and
active, but the hard gate is what matters. The outcome is still `0/100`
success, and the best gate-progress milestone is only `0.17` mean gates.

Next action: prepare v45 as a preflight/support lane. Keep hard eval on
unchanged `config/level3.toml`; keep inference as a single PPO Actor; build a
v5-compatible train-pool union retention dataset from disjoint seeds before
launching any v45 PPO training.
