## Interpretation

`value_target_scale=50.0` solved critic magnitude pressure:

```text
final value_loss: 0.0006676
final explained_variance: 0.8662
final value_targets_mean: -5.1409
final values_scaled_mean: -5.1382
```

But the policy drifted into aggressive spatial chasing:

```text
position and cross-track improved
velocity obedience collapsed
done rate worsened
reward worsened
action magnitude and action_delta rose
```

There is a confounder: `v62d_001` also changed PPO update pressure relative to
v62c:

```text
v62c: 4 minibatches, 1 update epoch
v62d_001: 8 minibatches, 4 update epochs
```

Therefore the next candidate should not immediately tune reward numbers. First
isolate whether the value-target-scale idea still fails under the conservative
v62c update schedule.
