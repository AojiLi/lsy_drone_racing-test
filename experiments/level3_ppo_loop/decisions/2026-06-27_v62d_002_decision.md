`value_target_scale=50.0` fixed critic scale well enough:

```text

final scaled values_mean: -5.3054
final value_targets_mean: -5.3080
```

But the policy still learned the wrong behavior:

```text

worse velocity obedience
higher done rate

```

The v62d_001 update-pressure confounder is now closed. Conservative PPO
settings improved v62d_002 relative to v62d_001, but did not beat the v62c 7M
frontier. The remaining blocker is not just critic scale; it is generic command
velocity obedience and action/safety drift.
