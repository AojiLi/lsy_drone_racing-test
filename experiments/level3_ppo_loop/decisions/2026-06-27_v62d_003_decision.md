## Interpretation

Doubling the generic velocity-error coefficient is not enough. The tracker
still does not reliably obey commanded speed, and the modest velocity gain at
20M comes with higher action_delta. The final checkpoint also drifts down from
the 20M milestone, so there is no reason to extend this candidate to 60M.

The local evidence points away from another blunt scalar reward increase and
toward the generator distribution: the policy likely needs clearer, more
balanced examples of constant-speed tracking, brake ramps, low-speed-through,
and recover-speed transitions.
